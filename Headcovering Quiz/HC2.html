Pauline Consistency Check

Does your view on head coverings match your faith?

5. Are Paul’s instructions binding for all churches as apostolic teachings?

5a. Is head covering optional or a church rule?

5b. Does obedience to Paul matter, or can we ignore it?

6a. Is head covering a veil or just hair?
